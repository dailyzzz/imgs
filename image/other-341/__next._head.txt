Four-panel grid meme adapting iconic 1988 John Carpenter film "They Live" where protagonist discovers special sunglasses revealing hidden messages and alien infiltration invisible to normal vision. Top right panel shows black background with white text at top: "YOU LIVE ON A SPINNING BALL". Below text displays photograph of Earth from space - classic "Blue Marble" style image showing full sphere with blue oceans, white cloud formations, green-brown continents clearly visible. Earth appears as globe floating in black space. This represents "normal" view that mainstream society accepts. Bottom left panel shows same man now fully wearing large black sunglasses covering eyes completely. Same plaid shirt and bookshelf background. Expression more serious/determined. Sunglasses represent "seeing the truth" - special vision that penetrates propaganda. In "They Live" film, glasses revealed subliminal messages like "OBEY" and "CONSUME" hidden in media/advertising. Here, metaphorically represents awakening to flat Earth "truth." Bottom right panel shows black background with white text at top: "THE EARTH IS FLAT AND STATIONARY". Map shows gridlines creating concentric circles and radial lines. Continents appear in tan/beige, oceans in blue-teal. This represents "hidden truth" supposedly revealed when viewing world through "awakened" lens. Lower right attribution: "fb.com/fematters". Format highly effective in conspiracy communities because it: 1) Frames belief as special knowledge/vision, 2) Positions believers as enlightened heroes, 3) Suggests mainstream view is programmed false reality, 4) Uses familiar pop culture reference making message accessible/shareable. "They Live" format particularly popular in conspiracy communities for topics like fake moon landings, chemtrails, 9/11 theories, COVID conspiracies - anything suggesting mainstream reality is fabricated illusion. Film's anti-establishment, anti-authority themes resonate with conspiracy worldview.

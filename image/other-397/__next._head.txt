WHY THIS ARGUMENT IS COMPLETELY WRONG: 1) CONSTANT VELOCITY FEELS LIKE STILLNESS: Physics principle of inertia - objects in constant motion remain in motion. Earth, atmosphere, rocks, everything moves TOGETHER at same velocity. There's no relative motion between rock pile and air around it. Analogy: Passenger on airplane traveling 500 mph can stack cards, pour drinks, walk around - because airplane, air inside, and passenger all move together. You only feel CHANGES in velocity (acceleration), not constant speed. 2) NO WIND FROM ROTATION: Atmosphere rotates WITH Earth - it's not stationary while Earth spins beneath it. Air at equator moves 1000 mph WITH ground, not PAST ground. No relative motion = no wind from rotation. Trade winds, weather patterns are caused by differential heating and Coriolis effect, not "Earth outrunning its atmosphere." 3) ORBITAL MOTION IS SMOOTH: Earth's orbit around Sun is essentially circular at constant speed. No acceleration = no felt force. We're in free-fall around Sun (orbit IS falling, but constantly missing). Gravity from Sun pulls Earth AND everything on it equally - no differential force to topple rocks. 4) INCREDIBLY GENTLE ROTATION: Earth's rotational speed at equator is 1000 mph, but rate of rotation is only 360 degrees per 24 hours = 0.00417 degrees per second. This is imperceptibly slow rotation. The "1000 mph" sounds fast but covers huge distance (circumference of Earth). Angular velocity is tiny. 5) SCALE OF MOTION: 67,000 mph orbital speed covers 584 million mile journey around Sun. Earth takes full year to complete orbit. Daily movement imperceptible at human scale.

WATER ARGUMENT ANALYSIS: Abizaid's argument represents classic flat Earth reasoning still used today: 1) "Water seeks its level" - True for small scales where gravity pulls water to lowest point in container. 2) "Water won't stay on round ball" - Misunderstands gravity. Gravity pulls toward center of mass, not "down" in absolute direction. On sphere, gravity pulls water toward center, holding it on surface regardless of position. 3) "Pan test" - Compares small container (where surface tension and small scale make water appear flat) to ocean (where Earth's gravity holds water to spherical surface). Scale difference makes comparison invalid. 4) "Ocean is level" - "Level" in physics means perpendicular to gravity vector, not geometrically flat. Ocean IS level - perpendicular to gravity at every point - but this means following Earth's curved surface, not being flat plane. SCIENTIFIC REBUTTAL: 1) GRAVITY: Water stays on spherical Earth because gravity pulls toward Earth's center. "Down" is always toward center, not absolute direction. Water on ball falls off because ball's mass is too small for significant gravity - not because round things can't hold water. Earth's gravity holds oceans firmly to surface regardless of location. 2) SCALE: At ocean scale, water DOES follow Earth's curvature - that's why ships disappear hull-first over horizon, why lighthouses have limited range, why sea-level varies with latitude (bulge at equator from rotation). Curvature too gradual to see in pan of water, but measurable over distances. 3) "LEVEL" DEFINITION: Surveyors and engineers understand "level" means perpendicular to gravity, not geometrically flat. Large construction projects (bridges, tunnels, railways) must account for Earth's curvature - proves spherical Earth assumed and verified in civil engineering. 4) OBSERVABLE EVIDENCE: Ships disappearing bottom-first, time zones existing, different stars visible at different latitudes, circular Earth shadow on moon during lunar eclipses, photos from space - all confirm spherical Earth, contradict flat Earth water argument. HISTORICAL CONTEXT: 1912 was not period of scientific ignorance - spherical Earth known for over 2,000 years, confirmed by centuries of global navigation, established in all scientific education. Flat Earth belief in 1912 was fringe position held by small group contradicting overwhelming scientific consensus. Abizaid's book represents pseudo-scientific contrarianism, not legitimate scientific debate.
